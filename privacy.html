🌱 Seed Quest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Seed Quest ("the Game") is a free browser-based casual game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The Game runs entirely in your browser and does not require registration, login, or any account creation.

The following data is stored locally on your device (via localStorage) and never sent to any server:

  • Best score (time survived)
  • Total tokens collected
  • Unlocked skins
  • Selected skin
  • Sound preference (on/off)

2. Advertising

The Game displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense or AdinPlay). These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads relevant to your interests. We do not control the data collected by ad networks.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Advertising Privacy

3. Children's Privacy

Seed Quest is designed for general audiences (all ages). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game does not include chat features, social networking, or user-generated content.

4. Third-Party Links

The Game does not contain links to third-party websites other than those related to advertising.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
